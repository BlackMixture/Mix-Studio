Video Models

LTX 2.3 25 fps · generates audio · text or image

The all-rounder. Two-stage generation (base + ×2 latent upscale) with Gemma prompt enhancement, straight from the Black Mixture workflow. The only engine that does text-to-video — no image needed. Fast (~1–3 min).

Supports: motion freedom · end frame · audio-driven · LoRAs · RTX 4K

10Eros DMD 24 fps · generates audio · image only

The motion specialist. TenStrip's 10Eros finetune of LTX 2.3 with a DMD distilled LoRA, Echo sampler and reference conditioning — motion that stays truer to your image, uncensored, and quick (~8 effective steps). The sigma presets change its character: DMD v1 is the balanced default, Model card follows the author's recommendation, V5 mix pushes a sharper upscale pass, Custom uses your schedules from Settings.

Supports: motion freedom · sigma presets · end frame · audio-driven · LoRAs · RTX 4K

Wan 2.2 16 fps · silent · image only

The heavyweight. Alibaba's 14B dual-expert model — excellent at complex motion, physics and prompt adherence, but slower and without audio. Runs in fast 4-step lightx2v mode by default; the Full quality toggle switches to 20 steps at cfg 3.5 for maximum fidelity (much slower). Generates at 16 fps — pick 32/48 fps · RIFE for smoother playback.

Supports: quality toggle · RIFE frame rates · LoRAs · RTX 4K

SCAIL 2 16 fps · motion-video audio · image + motion video

The motion transfer engine. Attach a source image and a motion video — SAM3 tracks the person in both, and SCAIL-2 (a Wan 2.1 finetune) re-animates your image with the clip's exact movement. Great for dance, action and performance transfer. Runs 6 steps with the lightx2v distill LoRA. Works best when both show a single, clearly visible person. Wan-family models generate at 16 fps — pick 32/48 fps · RIFE to interpolate extra frames for smoother playback.

Supports: motion video audio · Direct/Chunked length modes · RIFE frame rates · LoRAs · RTX 4K · up to 60s
