# Camera motion references

These MP4s are bundled motion guides for the Video tab's Camera Motion picker.

- The core pan, tilt, dolly, truck, pedestal, roll, and zoom clips are deterministic procedural guides generated by `scripts/generate-camera-motion-guides.py`.
- The `handheld-*` and `fpv-*` clips were supplied by the project owner from original generated footage. They were normalized to 960x512 H.264 without audio for lightweight browser previews and ComfyUI input.
- Rightward/clockwise variants of the captured clips are encoded horizontal mirrors of their corresponding leftward/counterclockwise source. They are separate files so the browser preview and ComfyUI guide always show the same direction.

The catalog and prompt wording live in `public/camera-motion.js`.
